📙 <b><u>FEATURES</u></b><br>
• create notes and to-do lists;<br>
• create categories to organize your notes, choose any color for the category;<br>
• autosave;<br>
• make a list numbered;<br>
• convert text notes into checklists and vice versa;<br>
• store your notes in a main list, archive, or trash.<br><br>

💡 <b><u>TOOLS</u></b><br>
• text search + search in several categories, found text is highlighted;<br>
• share notes — one or all at once;<br>
• pin favourites notes at the top of the list;<br>
• get full information about the content of the note: number of characters, words, lines;<br>
• sort checklist items alphabetically and by status.<br><br>

• categories order for categories sorting type.<br><br>

💜 <b><u>CUSTOMIZE</u></b><br>
• background color;<br>
• text size of title, content and description;<br>
• card appearance.<br><br>
